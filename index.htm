TRAVFITI

The different sticker pack

background

Travfiti is the brainchild of traveler myself, @3raxton. While travling through Europe this summer I saw multitudes of graffiti and I decided that I wanted to turn them into something that everybody could have access to. From this came Travfiti.

Why?

Being a UI Designer I decided that I wanted to try my hand at something I could design with the ability for it to be used anywhere on a device rather than something like an app. With these funny and weird graffiti designs, they can make messages pop or leave receivers completely confused.

When?

Travfiti is almost ready to be released. There are only a few more graffiti pieces to design and the purchase of an Apple Developer Certificate and Travfiti will come out of the works. If you would like to support this process or add your own graffiti, tweet me @3raxton
